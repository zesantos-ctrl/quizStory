Mini Story: A Day at the Park

Last Saturday, Emma woke up early because it was a sunny day. She decided to go to the park near her house. She took her backpack, a bottle of water, and a book.

When she arrived at the park, she saw many people: children playing football, families having picnics, and people walking their dogs. Emma sat under a big tree and started reading her book.

After twenty minutes, a small dog ran to her and sat next to her. Soon, the dog’s owner came and thanked Emma for staying with the dog.

Emma stayed in the park for two hours. She read, walked around, and enjoyed the fresh air. When the sun started to set, she returned home feeling relaxed and happy.
